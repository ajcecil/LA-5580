Liqure Purchasing (mL) in Iowa 2012-2025

The data in this Tableau Dashboard has been retrieved from Iowa Open Data!

This dashboard shows the purchasing habits of Iowans over the last 12 years, on a yearly and quarterly basis in milliliters (mL). This shows the distrubution of purchases across the state with notable increases in counties which contain recreation areas, such as Dickonson County which is home to lake Okaboji.
